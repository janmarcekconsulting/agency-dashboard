Chapter II

The Story of Hnxty

Hnxty was a 25-year-old man who struggled with money, health, mental health, and life in general. At 25, he made a decision that would change everything — he decided to transform himself completely.

He rebuilt his mental and physical health from the ground up. His body became lean, shredded, and full of energy. Hnxty became a man of quiet confidence — hard-working, stoic, with perfect posture, a symmetrical face, and a strong, masculine presence.

With his new identity came results. He scaled his agency to €5,000 per month and became a recognised professional in his niche — a well-known and respected name in the marketing industry. He then fixed his teeth and got veneers.

A few months later, he hit €10,000 per month — a defining milestone for any entrepreneur. He got his driving licence, bought his first car, and took a short road trip with friends to celebrate.

After the trip, he moved into a better two-bedroom flat — modern, quiet, and soundproof. He continued pushing and scaled his agency to €25,000 per month, living a healthy, fulfilled, and intentional life.

The final chapter of this phase: he saved up, bought a three-bedroom flat in Zelené Vlčince, and stepped into the next version of himself.
